Information Collected

Our Astha GPS service collects location data from tracking devices installed on cars. We do not collect data directly from the user's device.

This information is collected to provide users with the ability to view their car's location through the app.

How Information Is Used

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of providing users with the ability to track their car's location through our app.

Data Sharing

We do not share the collected information with any third-party service providers or entities. The location data collected is only accessible to the user through our app.

Security

We take appropriate measures to protect the security of the location data collected through our Astha GPS service. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
